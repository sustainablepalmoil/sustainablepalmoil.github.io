EcoSawit+ Certification

The EcoSawit+ Certification represents the gold standard in sustainable palm oil production. Designed to align with global benchmarks such as the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) and RSPO standards, our certification empowers Indonesian farmers while ensuring the highest levels of environmental stewardship and traceability.

Why Choose EcoSawit+ Certification?

  • Comprehensive Compliance: Meets and exceeds global sustainability standards, ensuring access to premium markets.
  • Advanced Traceability: Blockchain and satellite-based monitoring ensure full transparency from farm to market.
  • Empowered Farmers: Provides training, resources, and financial incentives to support sustainable practices and improve livelihoods.
  • Regenerative Practices: Focuses on restoring ecosystems, improving biodiversity, and promoting carbon sequestration.
