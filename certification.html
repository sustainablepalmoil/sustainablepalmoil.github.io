About EcoSawit+

The EcoSawit+ Certification represents the gold standard in sustainable palm oil production. It aligns with global benchmarks, such as the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR), empowering Indonesian farmers while ensuring the highest levels of environmental stewardship and traceability.

Key Benefits

  • Global Compliance: Meets stringent international sustainability standards.
  • Enhanced Transparency: Leverages blockchain and satellite monitoring for full traceability.
  • Farmer Empowerment: Provides training, resources, and incentives for sustainable practices.
  • Eco-Restoration: Focuses on biodiversity improvement and carbon sequestration.
