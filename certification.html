About EcoSawit+

The EcoSawit+ Certification represents the gold standard in sustainable palm oil production. It aligns with global benchmarks, such as the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR), empowering Indonesian farmers while ensuring the highest levels of environmental stewardship and traceability.

Key Benefits

Global Compliance

Meets stringent international sustainability standards.

Enhanced Transparency

Leverages blockchain and satellite monitoring for full traceability.

Farmer Empowerment

Provides training, resources, and incentives for sustainable practices.

Eco-Restoration

Focuses on biodiversity improvement and carbon sequestration.

Certification Process

1. Pre-Application Phase
Farmers fill out Form I (Basic Information) and Form II (Eligibility Checklist).
Authority: EcoSawit+ Registration Desk
↓
2. On-the-Ground GIS Assessment
EcoSawit+ Field Officer collects and verifies land data.
Authority: EcoSawit+ Field Team
↓
3. Satellite Verification
Cross-check submitted data with satellite imagery.
Authority: EcoSawit+ Compliance Team
↓
4. Audit and Reporting
Farmers receive the audit report with recommendations.
Authority: EcoSawit+ Auditors
↓
5. EcoSawit+ Certificate Granted subject to Independent Board Approval
↓
6. Market Access
Farmers use EcoSawit+ Label and connect with buyers.
Authority: EcoSawit+ Marketing Team
↓
7. Continuous Monitoring
Annual updates and reassessments every two years.
Authority: EcoSawit+ Monitoring Unit
