Membership Benefits

At Palm Eco Alliance, our membership provides exclusive access to resources and support for Indonesian farmers committed to sustainable palm oil production. As a member, you can access training, certification, and premium market opportunities.

Why Become a Member?

  • Premium Market Access: Gain access to international buyers who prioritise sustainability, including EU-regulated markets.
  • Training & Capacity Building: Benefit from workshops and tailored training programmes to improve yield and sustainability practices.
  • Financial Incentives: Access financial benefits like better pricing for your Certified palm oil.
  • EcoSawit+ Certification: Receive support to meet the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) compliance.
  • Networking: Join a collaborative network of like-minded farmers, industry experts, and sustainable agriculture advocates.

Who Can Join?

We welcome independent farmers, estates, and cooperatives dedicated to sustainable palm oil production and committed to meeting the highest environmental standards.

How to Apply?

Becoming a member is simple! Fill out the membership application form and our team will guide you through the next steps.
