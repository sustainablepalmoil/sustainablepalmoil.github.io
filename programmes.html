Our Programmes

Phase 1: Market Access and Onboarding

Farmers are enrolled to gain immediate access to premium markets. The onboarding process empowers members by enhancing their selling power through collective action.

Phase 2: Line of Credit Programme

After three months of membership, farmers become eligible for short-term credit lines to cover operational costs and boost productivity.

Phase 3: Capital Expenditure Programme

Once members have been active for nine months, they can access funds to make long-term investments in advanced machinery and infrastructure.

Compliance and Sustainability Support

With tools like satellite monitoring, blockchain traceability, and environmental analysis, we ensure all produce meets global standards, including the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR). This keeps members competitive in premium markets.
