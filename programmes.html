Our Programmes

Phase 1: Cooperative Buying Group Onboarding

Farmers are immediately enrolled in the buying group to enhance selling power from day one.

Phase 2: Line of Credit Programme

Members become eligible for short-term credit lines after three months of membership.

Phase 3: Capital Expenditure Programme

After nine months, members can access funds for long-term capital investments like machinery and infrastructure.

Compliance Support

We provide compliance tools, including satellite monitoring, blockchain traceability, and environmental analysis, ensuring all produce meets EUDR standards.
