About Palm Eco Alliance

At Palm Eco Alliance, we are redefining the future of palm oil production by empowering Indonesian farmers to achieve prosperity through sustainability. As a leader in the industry, we are committed to creating lasting value for farmers, the environment, and the global palm oil market.

Our Mission

To transform palm oil production in Indonesia by providing farmers with access to premium markets, fair pricing, and innovative solutions. Through cutting-edge technology, rigorous certifications, and comprehensive support, we aim to establish new benchmarks for sustainable practices.

What We Do

  • Empowering Farmers: We partner with farmers to improve yields, optimise practices, and enhance profitability through tailored training and financial incentives.
  • Leading with Innovation: Using advanced Geographic Information System (GIS) technology, satellite monitoring, and blockchain-based traceability, we ensure unmatched transparency and accountability.
  • Building Sustainable Futures: Promoting regenerative agricultural practices, protecting biodiversity, and enabling deforestation-free production for a sustainable supply chain.

Our Certification: Setting New Standards

The EcoSawit+ Certification sets a new benchmark for sustainable palm oil. Combining rigorous scientific standards with advanced monitoring systems, it ensures:

  • Full compliance with global standards such as the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) and RSPO requirements.
  • Traceability of palm oil from farm to market, using blockchain and satellite technology.
  • Responsible land management that protects biodiversity and promotes environmental stewardship.
  • Access to premium markets that value sustainability and traceability.
