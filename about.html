About the Cooperative

Our mission is to empower Indonesian palm oil farmers through collective action. We provide access to financing, better prices, and sustainable market opportunities, ensuring compliance with global standards like the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR).

Formed to support sustainable palm oil production, our cooperative unites farmers under shared goals of fair pricing, compliance, and responsible land management.
