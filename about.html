About Palm Eco Alliance

At Palm Eco Alliance, we are redefining the future of palm oil production by empowering Indonesian farmers to achieve prosperity through sustainability. As a leader in the industry, we are committed to creating lasting value for farmers, the environment, and the global palm oil market.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the Indonesian palm oil industry complies with the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR), enabling farmers and plantations to maintain access to the EU market while securing better prices for sustainably produced palm oil.

What We Do

Empowering Farmers

We partner with farmers to improve yields, optimise practices, and enhance profitability through tailored training and financial incentives.

Leading with Innovation

Using advanced Geographic Information System (GIS) technology, satellite monitoring, and blockchain-based traceability, we ensure unmatched transparency and accountability.

Building Sustainable Futures

Promoting regenerative agricultural practices, protecting biodiversity, and enabling deforestation-free production for a sustainable supply chain.

Our Certification: Setting New Standards

The EcoSawit+ Certification sets a new benchmark for sustainable palm oil. Using advanced monitoring systems, it ensures:

EUDR Compliance

Full compliance with the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR).

Full Traceability

Traceability of palm oil from farm to market, using GIS assessment and satellite technology.

Environmental Stewardship

Responsible land management that protects biodiversity and promotes environmental stewardship.

Market Access

Ensures access to premium markets that value sustainability and traceability.
