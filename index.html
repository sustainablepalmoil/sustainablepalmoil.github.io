Welcome to the Future of Palm Oil Farming

Empower your farm. Increase your profits. Secure your future.

As a member of the Sustainable Palm Oil Cooperative, you’re not just joining an organisation—you’re joining a movement that puts farmers first. Our cooperative is designed to uplift independent farmers across Indonesia by providing access to premium markets, fair pricing, and essential resources to ensure long-term sustainability and profitability.

Why Join Us?

Better Prices, Bigger Markets

Access high-value international markets that reward sustainable practices. Our cooperative ensures compliance with global standards, including the EU Deforestation Regulation, so you can sell to premium markets at fair prices without the hassle.

Affordable Financing for Growth

Need funds to expand, improve, or innovate? As a member, you’ll qualify for exclusive credit lines and capital investment programs tailored to support your farm’s growth and productivity.

Support Every Step of the Way

You’re not alone. Our team offers guidance in sustainable farming practices, certification support, and compliance monitoring to keep your farm aligned with the highest environmental and quality standards.

Collective Power for Lasting Impact

By joining forces with farmers like you, we’re stronger together. With collective bargaining and shared resources, we reduce costs, enhance negotiating power, and maximise benefits for all members.

This is your chance to thrive in a rapidly changing industry. Don’t miss the opportunity to build a secure and profitable future with the support you deserve.
